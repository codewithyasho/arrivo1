Pioneering Capabilities

Key Platform Features

Harnessing cutting-edge AI camera vision to simplify smart parking deployment, reduce capex, and maximize enforcement efficiency.

Core Strength

AI Camera Vision-Based Occupancy Monitoring

Our standout feature. We replace traditional, expensive, battery-draining ground sensors with highly intelligent overhead camera surveillance system.

  • 100% Camera-Based: No sensor dependency resulting in lower capex & zero ground drilling maintenance.
  • Real-time Accuracy: Slot-level detection using optimized AI models at the edge or cloud.
  • Rich Analytics: Generates automated heatmaps, dwell-time analysis, and parking violations.
  • Versatile Use: Fits both congested on-street roads and multi-level off-street complexes.
Core Strength

App-Based Parking Enforcement

Equip parking wardens and city municipal teams with powerful mobile tools connected directly to the camera vision framework.

  • Ground Enforcement App: Seamless notification stream alerting wardens of illegal/overtime parkers.
  • E-Challan Workflow: Instantly issue digital fines with precise photo and video evidence tags.
  • Automatic Blacklists: Track repeat offenders and sound alarms on unauthorized vehicle entry.
  • Verification Logs: Cloud-backed, legally compliant audit trails to settle public disputes.

Smart Parking Discovery

Locate nearby spots instantly. Filter by price, vehicle type, walking distance, and active availability.

Advance Reservation

Schedule your hourly, daily, or monthly slot. Supports corporate bookings, VIP reservation tags, and logistics fleets.

FASTag & Cashless Payments

Leverage pre-linked FASTag transponders for touchless entry/exit. Full support for UPI, wallets, and cards.

Central Command Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard showing live utilization, active camera feeds, revenue reporting, and operational health.

Hardware Integration

Full support for barrier gates, ANPR (license plate) optical readers, and portable ticketing devices.

About arrivo

arrivo (by Avenir Mobility) is a Pune-based smart parking and mobility technology company specializing in AI camera vision systems, FASTag-based automation, and app-driven parking enforcement.

We help smart cities, airports, ports, malls, and infrastructure projects monitor parking occupancy in real time, enforce regulations efficiently, and deliver seamless parking experiences to users.

Our camera-first approach eliminates dependency on ground sensors, significantly reducing deployment and maintenance costs while improving accuracy and scalability.
